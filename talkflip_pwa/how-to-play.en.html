How to Play

No install — just a link. Pick the number of players, choose a mode and a deck, then flip cards one at a time.

Basic flow

  • ① Players — set how many of you are playing (solo, two, or a group).
  • ② Mode — decide how you'll play.
  • ③ Deck — pick from Warm Up, Funny, Deep, Couple, Party, or Spicy.
  • ④ Flip a card — answer, vote, or pass if it misses!

Want to jump in fast? Tap Quick start next to the player count to begin instantly with a recommended setup.

Modes

Who's Most Likely

Read “Who here is most likely to…?” and everyone points to one person. The most-voted person gives a short defense for extra fun. 3+ players.

This or That

Everyone picks A or B. The minority side explains first. 1+ players.

Classic Flip

Take turns answering questions. Great for first meetings and casual settings. 1+ players.

Hot Seat

One person sits in the “hot seat.” Draw 3 cards, answer 2, then pass to the next person. 2+ players.

Taboo Round

One person describes a topic without saying the banned words. Everyone else catches slips. 3+ players.

Language

Choose Korean / English in Settings.

Cards you love

Save them with the heart and share them with friends. You can also share a recap when a round ends.

Tips to liven up a gathering

  • Start light with Warm Up or Funny.
  • Once the energy is up, use Who's Most Likely or Taboo for reactions.
  • For couples, the Couple deck helps you learn more about each other.
  • Never force an answer — just pass if it misses!
