Idea Board

One question from you could become the next card. Great ideas get added to the game after review.

TalkFlip grows with its players. If you've ever thought "this question would be fun" at a hangout, with a partner, or among friends, send it below. If it's picked, you'll meet it inside the game.

What we're looking for

  • Light questions anyone can answer in about three seconds
  • No "right answer" — prompts that spark reactions
  • Funny, curious, or a slightly tough choice
  • No attacks on real people, and no sensitive topics like appearance, income, politics, or religion
